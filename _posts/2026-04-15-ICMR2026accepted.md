2026年に開催されるマルチメディア検索のトップカンファレンスである16th ACM International Conference on Multimedia Retrieval（ICMR2026）にて、M2大江、M1杉田がRegular Track、M2金田がBrave New Ideas Trackで3件の論文がフルペーパーとして採択されました。

![写真](/assets/img/posts/20260415/ICMR2026.png "papers")

- **Asymmetric Pipeline for Dataset Construction and Situation-aware Generative Outfit Retrieval Leveraging Differences in Task Difficulty**, Yuma Oe, Katsumi Tanaka, Yoshiyuki Shoji, Proc. of The 16th ACM International Conference on Multimedia Retrieval (ICMR2026), to appear, 2026.

- **Retrieval of LoRA Models based on Layer-Wise Weight Embedding without Metadata**, Yuro Kanada, Yuma Oe, Huu-Long Pham, Makoto P. Kato, Hiroaki Ohshima, Sumio Fujita and Yoshiyuki Shoji, Proc. of The 16th ACM International Conference on Multimedia Retrieval (ICMR2026), to appear, 2026.

- **Which LoRA Should Be Merged Next? Retrieving an Additional LoRA from a Target Image**, Daichi Sugita, Huu-Long Pham, Hiroaki Ohshima, Sumio Fujita and Yoshiyuki Shoji, Proc. of The 16th ACM International Conference on Multimedia Retrieval (ICMR2026), to appear, 2026.

ICMR2026については[こちら](https://icmr2026.org/index.html)。

2026年6月にオランダのアムステルダムにて発表予定です。
